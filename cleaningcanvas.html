How to Clean Canvas Shoes


Step 1: Prep your shoes for cleaning

Remove the laces from your shoes. Mix warm water with a bit of dish soap and grab an old tooth brush. Be sure to also grab a towel to wipe down each shoe.
Step 2: Wipe down your shoes Wipe off any excess dirt from your shoe with a paper towel. Then, dip the toothbrush and gently scrub the entire shoe from the outside including the sole and tongue. Scrub the shoe gently and avoid pressing too hard. Make sure to wipe down the shoe to get rid of the water as best as you can before letting them air dry.
Step 3: Cleaning the laces Mix a little bleach into the solution and drop the laces in for about 30 minutes to an hour. If your laces are any other color besides white, do not mix bleach with the laces. Instead, just soak them in warm water with dish soap. Let the shoe and the laces dry thorougly before going on to the next step.
Step 4: Relacing your shoes Make sure everything has dried before moving on to this step. Pull the tongue of your shoe in place and then (starting from the bottom) relace your shoes. You can relace them the same way they came out of the box (factory laced) or customize them however you'd like. Depending on the style you are lacing, pay attention to the tongue and its placement in the shoe.

Keep in mind:
1. Be gentle with the shoe
2. Use bleach only for white laces
3. Make sure to let the shoes dry completely before lacing them
